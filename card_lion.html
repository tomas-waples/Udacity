Lion

A lion can run for short distances at 50 mph and leap as far as 36 feet.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 4.6 to 8.2 Feet
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: The African Savanna

The lion was once found throughout Africa, Asia and Europe but now exists only in Africa with one exception. The last remaining Asiatic lions are found in Sasan-Gir National Park in India, which was primarily created to protect the species. Currently, there are approximately 350-400 lions in the parks.
